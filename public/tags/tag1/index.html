When you think of network storage, you‚Äôre probably thinking of a NAS, since that‚Äôs what most YouTubers show off. But there are actually two different types of network storage:

SAN (Storage Area Network)

  • Block-level storage for direct connections over the local network.
  • Typically uses iSCSI over Ethernet or Fiber.
